THE COMPANY

Company Profile

Founded over 50 years ago, Super Rich Thailand is the most trusted currency exchange leader in Thailand as it is one of the very first privately-owned companies to be granted official permission to operate currency exchange business in the Kingdom.

We have gone from strength to strength since our establishment. Our dedicated and experienced staff are highly trained to ensure you receive a first-class service.

Offering the best possible exchange rates in the Thai market, we pride ourselves on our drive to satisfy your needs, whether you are a tourist or a multinational company. Our one-stop service centre is able to cater to both regular customers as well as those who walk in.
